Decision-support map for matching council services to technology patterns and SenSen product opportunities.

⌂ Council Services

Executive guide

This map helps council leaders scan the full service portfolio and identify where technology patterns and SenSen products may support operational improvement, service redesign, compliance, evidence capture, or city intelligence.

How to use: start with a service domain, inspect the right panel, then use Technology Patterns or SenSen Product Lens to test fit.

Decision signals added

  • Plain-English labels: Service Categories and Specific Services.
  • Governance-sensitive services are flagged.
  • Top opportunities are included as prompts for discussion.

SenSen product lens

The product section is intentionally kept as a SenSen-focused operational lens: SenFORCE, SenPIC, SenMAP, SenTRACK, SenIQ, SenCIP and SenBOTS.

Use this lens as an opportunity-mapping aid. Final fit still needs procurement, privacy, cyber, records and governance review.
